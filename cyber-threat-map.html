Cyber Threat Map

Project Overview

"Cyber Threat Map" is a real-time visualization tool designed to display global cyber attack data as it happens. The goal is to help blue teams, analysts, and decision-makers monitor threat trends, geolocation of attacks, and TTPs (tactics, techniques, procedures) used in real-world scenarios.
